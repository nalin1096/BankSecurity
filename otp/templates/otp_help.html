Important Instructions*
  1. For added secuirty, you are provided with a facility of adding OTP functions. These functions will be used at the time of Login, where you will be give a random OTP generated by the bank and you have to proivde the modified OTP based on your functions for each digit.
  2. You can use two operands (explained in point 3) per digit in your OTP fucntion. You are also provided with a set of modifiers:
    1. 9's Complement (~)
    2. 10's Complement (-)
    3. Square (^2)
    4. Square of 9's complement(~^2)
    5. Square of 10's Complement (-^2)
    10's Complement of 0 is 0 and the resulting digit of each function is %10 of the answer.
  3. Format for the operand is as follows:
    1. 1st Digit to 6th Digit represents 1st to 6th Digit of the Bank generated random OTP.
    2. d1, d2 represent the 1st and 2nd digit of the current date (if the day is single digit d1 = 0)
    3. m1, m2 represent the 1st and 2nd digit of the current month (if the month is single digit m1 = 0)
    4. min1, min2 represent the first and the second smallest digits in the bank generated random OTP respectively
*OTP functions for each digit and as a whole are optional.
